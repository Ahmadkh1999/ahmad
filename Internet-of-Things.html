Internet of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to the network of interconnected devices embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies that enable them to collect and exchange data. These devices range from everyday objects such as smart thermostats, wearable fitness trackers, to industrial machinery and vehicles. IoT allows these devices to communicate and share information, facilitating automation, data analysis, and improved efficiency in various sectors.
